Mechanical Keyboard

Social Media Ad Concept

A conceptual social media advertisement designed for a mechanical keyboard product named Sakura. This project explores visual branding and aesthetic-led marketing for tech products, delivered in both static and motion formats.

Role: Graphic Designer (Concept, Layout, Motion Adaptation)

Objective

To create a scroll-stopping social media ad that blends technology with soft floral visuals, creating a unique identity while maintaining clarity in a fast-paced feed environment.

Design Approach:

  • - Visual-first marketing style
  • - Supporting elements to reinforce product name and mood
  • - Clean typography hierarchy
  • - Balanced negative space
  • - Subtle feature and compatibility text to avoid clutter
  • - Adaptation into motion format for higher engagement
