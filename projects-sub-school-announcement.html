Computer Engineering General Meeting Announcement

Social Media Graphic

Goal

Design a social media graphic aimed at increasing student engagement for an upcoming general meeting, using organization related 3D elements to make it visually appealing.

Role and Collaboration: Sole graphic designer. Communicated with technical senior vice president and technical members for feedback and information.

Concept

  • - Matching 3D visuals for computer engineering program
  • - Professional yet approachable style
  • - Clear communication of date, time, and purpose of the meeting

Design Elements / Approach

  • - Subtle 3D elements like abstract shapes, or icons
  • - Clean typography for readability
  • - Balanced composition to maintain a techy yet minimalistic feel

Outcome / Result

Engaging social media post that encourages student participation. Modern, professional, and interactive feel suitable for the audience.
