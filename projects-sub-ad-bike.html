Bike

Social Media Ad Concept

A conceptual social media advertisement developed for a bike product named Shift. The campaign promotes the Shift model as a practical transportation alternative.

Role: Graphic Designer (Concept, Layout, Motion Adaptation)

Objective

To design a clean and modern social media advertisement that presents a bicycle as a convenient and low-maintenance transportation option using simple and direct messaging.

Design Approach:

  • - Minimal and modern layout
  • - Emphasis on practicality and sustainability
  • - Strong contrast for mobile readability
  • - Simple messaging suited for fast-scrolling platforms
  • - Visual balance between product and text
