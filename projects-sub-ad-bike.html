Bikke "Shift" Bicycle

Concept Social Media Ad

A conceptual social media advertisement for a bycicle named "Shift". The project focuses on positioning the bike as a practical, low-maintenance transportation alternative. This project shows how simple, direct messaging can communicate with the benefits and appeal of sustainable travel to a modern audience.

Role: Graphic Designer (Concept, Layout, Motion Adaptation)

Tool: Canva

Objective

To design a clean and modern advertisement that highlights the "Shift" as a convenient transportation option. The aim was to use a minimalist style that delivers a clear messahge quickly to audience.

Design Approach:

  • - Minimal and modern layout
  • - Emphasis on practicality and sustainability
  • - Strong contrast for mobile readability
  • - Simple messaging suited for fast-scrolling platforms
  • - Visual balance between product and text
