Blue Minimalist Scrapbook Photo Card

Personal Graphic Design / Gift

Goal

Design a minimalist, scrapbook-style photo card to gift a friend, combining simplicity with a creative personal touch.

Role: Graphic designer.

Concept

  • - Minimalist aesthetic with scrapbook vibes
  • - Blue tones for a calm, friendly feel
  • - Balance between decorative elements and photo focus

Approach

  • - Cool blue color palette for visual harmony
  • - Clean typography and subtle scrapbook elements (tape, textures)
  • - Thoughtful layout to keep the photo as the main focus

Result

Personalized, stylish photo card that feels cozy and minimalist, perfect as a creative gift.

Print Preparation

Print-ready A4 version with cut marks and bleed setup.
