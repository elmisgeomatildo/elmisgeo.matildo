Student Council Information & Display Board Design

Bulletin Board

Overview

A printed bulletin board that presents organizational identity, leadership structure, and key information, while providing space for future announcements.

Rationale

The previous bulletin board did not effectively represent the identity of the Computer Engineering council and lacked strong visual distinction within the campus environment. A redesign was necessary to create a clearer, more cohesive visual representation of the organization while improving its visibility and overall presentation.

Goal

Revamp the existing bulletin board to better reflect the Computer Engineering councilâ€™s identity while creating a visually distinctive design that stands out within the campus environment.

Role and Collaboration: Sole graphic designer. Communicated with the council president and technical senior vice president for feedback.

Tool: Affinity Designer

Reference

Rough sketch

Previous version of bulletin board

Design Process & Development

1. Wireframe Creation

Built the wireframe using the sketch and old bulletin board as size and layout reference.

2. Wireframe Adjustment

Refined the wireframe after updated dimensions were provided.

2.1 Name Plate Variations

Designed 5 themed name plate styles. Design 4 was selected.

3. Initial Layout

Applied the approved name plates to the layout.

3.1 Featured Council Element

Issue: The bulletin board needed a stronger visual presence to stand out among other campus displays.

So multiple standout layout concepts for the core council members was developed. Design 3 was chosen.

4. Final Layout Structure

Rearranged the layout to integrate the new featured element.

5. Text Headers Added

Inserted section headers including achievements, activities, announcements, and committees.

6. Content Styling & Spaces

Added custom styling for text sections and blank areas for future announcements.

7. Print Prep & Visual Polish

Applied bleed settings, background design, shadows, and gradients for depth.

8. Corner Enhancements

Added corner graphics to balance and fill empty spaces.

9. Final Design

Completed and finalized the bulletin board for printing.
