Bookk Fligh Booking Service

Concept Social Media Ad

A conceptual social media advertisement for a concept travel booking service called "Bookk". This project highlights affordable travel and a fast booking service, encouraging users to explore more while spending less. I focused on creating a design the feels familiar and trustworthy.

Role: Graphic Designer (Concept, Layout, Motion Adaptation)

Tool: Canva

Objective

To design an advertisement that clearly communicates affordability and ease of use. The aim was to balance "wanderlust" visuals with direct, functional information so users can understand the value of the service at a glance.

Design Approach:

  • - Travel-themed visuals to spark wanderlust
  • - Clean, modern layout
  • - Emphasis on affordability and speed
  • - High contrast text for mobile readability
  • - Designed for fast-scrolling social media feeds
