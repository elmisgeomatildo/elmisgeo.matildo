Flight Booking Service

Social Media Ad Concept

A conceptual social media advertisement created for a mock flight booking service called Bookk. The campaign highlights affordable travel and fast booking, encouraging users to explore the world without overspending.

Role: Graphic Designer (Concept, Layout, Motion Adaptation)

Objective

To design a social media advertisement that communicates affordability, speed, and ease of booking, while maintaining a clean and engaging layout suitable for digital platforms.

Design Approach:

  • - Travel-themed visuals to spark wanderlust
  • - Clean, modern layout
  • - Emphasis on affordability and speed
  • - High contrast text for mobile readability
  • - Designed for fast-scrolling social media feeds
