Minimalist Abstract Photo Card

Personal Graphic Design / Gift

Front

Back

Goal

Create a visually striking, abstract photo card for a friend to place in their ID case, combining a minimalist style with modern glass-inspired effects.

Role: Graphic designer.

Concept

  • - Abstract, glassy, and modern aesthetic
  • - Cyan color palette for a cool and vibrant feel
  • - Minimalist layout to highlight shapes and textures

Approach

  • - Clean geometric and abstract forms
  • - Glass-inspired transparency and layering effects
  • - Minimalist typography or no text to focus on visuals

Result

Eye-catching, personal card suitable for display in an ID case.

Print Preparation

Print-ready A4 version with cut marks and bleed setup.
