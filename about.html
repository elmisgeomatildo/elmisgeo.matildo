About Me

I'm a graphic design-focused creative with a growing background in IT and administrative support. I create clean, practical visual materials while also offering technical and organizational assistance when needed. My goal is not just to make things look good, but to make them work better for the people using them.

Graphic Design

I design visual content that is clear, simple, and purposeful. My work includes social media graphics, posters, promotional materials, layout design, and other visual assets for digital use. I focus on designs that communicate ideas effectively without unnecessary clutter.

IT & Technical Skills

Alongside design, I have hands-on experience with basic hardware and software troubleshooting, device setup, and general system maintenance. I'm comfortable exploring new tools and technologies and can adapt quickly to different technical environments.

Administrative Support

I also provide support with basic administrative tasks such as data entry, file organization, and documentation work. I'm familiar with common office tools and understand the importance of keeping information structured, manageable and confidential.

Work Approach

I am open to feedback and revisions, and I value improving my work through collaboration. I learn new tools and systems quickly and aim to produce outputs that are not only visually appealing but also functional and easy to use.

Who I Work Best With

I'm a good fit for small businesses, startups, and individuals who need both creative design and practical technical or admin support in one person.
