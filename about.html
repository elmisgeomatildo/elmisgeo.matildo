About me

I bridge the gap between creative design and technical support. With a background in Graphic Design, IT, and Admin Support, I focus on creating engaging visuals while ensuring the systems and organization behind them work reliably.

What I Bring To The Table:

  • Design with Purpose: I create social media graphics, posters, and layouts. I enjoy switching between clean, simple designs and more detailed, vibrant visuals depending on what the projects' needs.
  • Technical and Hardware Support: I have hands-on experience with hardware, including replacing faulty or broken components in devices. While I'm always learning, I'm comfortable with troubleshooting software issues and attempting repairs to get devices back in working order.
  • Organizational Support: I focus on keeping information structured and manageable. I can handle essential tasks like data entry , file organization, and documentation that allow a project to run smoothly from start to finish.

My Work Style

I work best in roles that require a mix of creative and technical skills. I value feedback, I'm quick to learn new software, and I prioritize making things functional and easy to use for everyone involved.
