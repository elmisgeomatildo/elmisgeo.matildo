Greeting & Thanksgiving Announcement

Social Media Graphic

Goal

Create an energetic and lively graphic evoking warmth and positivity with a summer/beach theme.

Role and Collaboration: Graphic designer. Communicated with technical senior vice president and technical members for feedback and information.

Concept

  • - Summer and beach vibes: sun, sand, waves, bright colors
  • - Cheerful, welcoming tone to energize the audience
  • - Quick and easily digestible visual for social media

Design Elements / Approach

  • - Bright, warm color palette (yellows, blues, oranges)
  • - Playful typography and beach imagery
  • - Simple layout to emphasize the message

Outcome / Result

A clear and visually appealing graphic designed for an energetic and engaging social media post.
